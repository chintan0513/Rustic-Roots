About Rustic Roots

At Rustic Roots, we specialize in transforming your celebrations into unforgettable experiences. Based in Toronto, our passion lies in crafting bespoke decorations that reflect your unique story and style.

Founded by Rajan Trivedi and Shrinkhla Pohre, who bring over 7 years of experience in event management, our team is dedicated to delivering exceptional service and stunning designs for every occasion.
